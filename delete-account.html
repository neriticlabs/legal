Delete Your Account

MantaForge — Forge Your Body
Last updated: 26 March 2026

You can permanently delete your MantaForge account and all associated data directly from within the app. No need to contact support.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MantaForge app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right corner.
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Fitness profile and quiz answers
  • All workout plans and workout history
  • All nutrition plans and meal logs
  • Water intake records
  • Community posts and kudos
  • Friend group memberships
  • Streak and trophy data

What Is Retained

  • A one-way SHA-256 hash of your email address is retained to prevent re-registration with a deleted account. Your original email cannot be recovered from this hash.
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (not linked to your identity) may be retained for up to 14 months.
Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. We cannot recover your account or any associated data after deletion. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through your device's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store) before deleting your account to avoid being charged after deletion.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, contact us at support@neriticlabs.com and we will process your request.
